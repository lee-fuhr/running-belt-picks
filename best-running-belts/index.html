A good running belt does more than hold your phone – it stays put through intervals, keeps your keys from jangling, and lets you carry water without one hand locked on a bottle. The right one disappears on your waist and gives you quick access to essentials, so you can focus on your stride instead of your gear. Look for a belt that fits snugly without riding up, has enough pocket space for your phone and nutrition, and offers a bottle that doesn’t slosh or pop out mid-run. Some designs favor minimal bounce, others prioritize carrying capacity, and a few let you swap between bottles and flasks. Your choice should match the type of running you do most often.
